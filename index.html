WC-Car

WebRTC controlled (RC) car

The Idea

RC Car + Smartphone --> WebRTC --> Smartphone/PC/Whatever to control it --> have fun!!!

The Team

  • Lisa Maria Lederer
  • Sebastian Slamanig
  • Michael Poschacher

The Reason

Currently we are studying MultiMediaTechnology at the FH-Salzburg. This project is our third project in the course of our bachelor program, the target is to strenghen our knowledge of Web Technologies. That's why we've decided to use WebRTC (currently a RFC of the W3C) for controlling a RC car. (And... it's fun :)).
